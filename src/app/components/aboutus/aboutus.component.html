All in One Austasia The Unique Place to be!

In May 2001 Austasia indoor cricket stadium was inaugurated with two courts. During a period of 13 months this was extended to a fully fledged sports & leisure complex encompassing a number of other sporting & recreational activities. At present Austasia International sports complex has over 7500 who are enjoying various facilities that are offered by Austasia. It has grown from an indoor cricket stadium to a life style builder for the members & Others, especially for the youth in the country and now to the Asian region as many visit the country to train and play in different tournaments in different sports from c ountries like India, Nepal, Pakistan, UAE, Australia, New Zealand, England and South Africa. The complex has introduced many new sports and some change the version of sports as per the world trend to suit the requirement of the youth and the society.
